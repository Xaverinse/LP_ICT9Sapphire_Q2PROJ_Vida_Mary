Hot Chocolate


INGREDIENTS

1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
½ cup unsweetened cocoa
1 ½ teaspoons vanilla extract
⅛ teaspoon salt
6 ½ cups hot water
3 mini marshmallows
1 candy cane

STEPS

Combine sweetened condensed milk, cocoa, vanilla, and salt in a large saucepan over medium heat; mix well. Slowly stir in hot water; cook until heated through, stirring occasionally. Do not boil. Top with marshmallows and candycane. Store covered in the refrigerator.
